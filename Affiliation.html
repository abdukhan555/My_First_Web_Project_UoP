The Affiliation Committee

The Affiliation Committee of the University of Peshawar consist of:

  1. The Vice Chancellor (Convener);
  2. One member of the Syndicate to be nominated by the Syndicate;
  3. Nominee of the Academic Council;
  4. The Director of Higher Education (Colleges), Khyber Pakhtunkhwa;
  5. The Registrar (Member/ Secretary).
