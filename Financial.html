The Finance and Planning Committee

The Finance and Planning Committee of the University of Peshawar consist of:

  1. The Vice-Chancellor (Chairperson);
  2. A representative of the Higher Education Commission (HEC);
  3. One member each of the Syndicate and the Senate to be appointed by the Syndicate and the Senate respectively;
  4. The  Secretary  to  Government,  Higher  Education  Department  or  his nominee not below the rank of an Additional Secretary;
  5. The Secretary to Government, Finance Department or his nominee not below the rank of an Additional  Secretary;
  6. The Director P & D;
  7. The Director Finance/ Treasurer (Secretary).
