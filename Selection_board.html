The Selection Board

The Selection Board of the University of Peshawar consist of:

  1. The Vice Chancellor  who shall be the Chairperson
  2. The Dean/ senior most teacher of the Faculty concerned;
  3. The Chairperson of the Teaching Department /Director of the Institute concerned;
  4. The Chairman or a member of the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Public Service Commission to be nominated by the Chairman;
  5. Two persons of eminence to be appointed by the Syndicate
  6. The Registrar of the University (Secretary).
